Who I am

I'm a product designer with a focus on reducing friction — in interfaces, in decision-making, and in how teams think about design problems.

My process starts with understanding behaviour. I spend time in research not just to validate ideas, but to uncover what people are actually trying to do. From there, I work in structured iterations — from rough flows to high-fidelity prototypes, testing each step against real feedback.

I've designed across creator monetisation platforms and shared living tools: problems that sit at the intersection of psychology, coordination, and product strategy. What I've learned: the best design rarely adds. It clarifies.
